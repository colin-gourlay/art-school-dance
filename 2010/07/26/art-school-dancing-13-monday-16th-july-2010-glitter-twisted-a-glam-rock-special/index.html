This is an archive of the show notes for Art School Dancing, a radio programme hosted by the late Ally Gourlay. The show notes are gathered from Facebook posts to the Art School Dancing Facebook Group. Some of the shows are available to stream on mixcloud: https://www.mixcloud.com/unlistenablerubbish/

ART SCHOOL DANCING #13 – GLITTER & TWISTED – A GLAM ROCK SPECIAL

Hey everyone, dig out the platform shoes, get the glitter on the cheeks and prepare for ‘Glitter And Twisted’ tonight on Art School Dancing. Full to the brim with requests , tonight will feature T. Rex, David Bowie, Roxy Music, The Jook, Wizzard, Gary Glitter, Ayshea, Simon Turner, The Sweet, Slade, Elton John, Queen, The Glitter Band, Brian Eno, Mott The Hoople, Sparks, Cockney Rebel, Mud, The Arrows and Fox…be sure to tune in tonight at 10pm on Leith FM (98.8 in Edinburgh area and online)
