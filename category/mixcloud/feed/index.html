There’s a session recorded yesterday by Becky Becky especially for tomorrow night’s programme, plus a track, also recorded yesterday especially for the programme by Smallgang’s Simon Kobayashi. There are interviews with Wire, Woodpecker Wooliams, Hannah Peel, and smallgang Sessions tracks from across the years by Strawberry Whiplash, J. Mascis, The Aphrodisiacs, The Motion and Roberta Pia, along with some vintage Ally cocking things up from 2004. There’s also music from artists who have become great friends such as the Cuban Boys, Bubblegum Lemonade and Antony Harding. Throw in a couple of obligatory Roy Wood tracks too, and the best of the 21st Century and it may run slightly over the four hours but who’s counting eh? But most of all, I hope you’re all there because, without you, Art School Dancing would never be the programme it has been. Ally xx
