Dear Friends. All good things must come to an end, and so, with a heavy heart, it’s time for one last hurrah! Monday 30th March will be the final Art School Dancing, and as such will be a four hour programme running from 8pm-midnight on K107FM. The decision to end is not one I have taken lightly, after all, how easily can one bring to an end a programme which has been running on various radio stations since July 1984? However, with my illness starting to take its toll, recently the time it takes to put a programme together has increased and has at times been a struggle to get through. Your support and commitment has helped me no end. With that in mind, I never want to find myself in a position where I can’t do the programme and your good selves justice, despite my plethora of weekly cock-ups. It also makes sense to end on a high, at a time when the programme is attracting record listener numbers. Leave ’em wanting more as someone once said. I also wanted to be in a position to choose when the programme came to an end rather than have my health make the decision for me. Taking all recent factors into account, I have been planning to bring the programme to a conclusion at some point in the near future, and having thought about my own “performance” last night, I feel that now is the right time. I hope you’ll join me next Monday (30th) for the last ever Art School Dancing. I’ll be playing my own favourites from 1965-2015 and also hope to put in some rather special pieces from across the programmes 31 year history. Meanwhile I’d like to thank K107FM, and all the stations which have hosted the programme over the years for allowing me free rein to play the music I like. I’d like to thank the many artists who have contributed to the programme over the years for their unstiniting determination to get their music heard in a sea of corporate and mainstream mediocrity. And finally thank YOU, the heartbeat of the programme, the faithful listening public who have stuck with the programme over the years, have voted in the Hogmanay Hundreds, and given me support and faith in the music. I’ll forever be in your debt. Love Ally. ART SCHOOL DANCING – THE LAST EVER DANCE IN THE ASYLUM – MONDAY 30th MARCH 8pm-MIDNIGHT on K107FM and online.

Something special is happening for Monday’s final Art School Dancing. My wonderful friends are recording a session especially for the final programme. I am indeed truly humbled. My sincere thanks and love to Pete & Gemma x
